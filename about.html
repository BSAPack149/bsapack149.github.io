About Pack 149

Pack 149 has been calling Spicewood Elementary home for over 40 years. Chartered by the Northwest Austin Rotary Club, we are one of the largest and most active packs in the Chisholm Trail District of the Capitol Area Council. More details coming soon. Contact us for more information.
