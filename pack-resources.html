Health & Safety

Annual Health & Medical Record — Parts A & B

RequiredOvernight ActivitiesCampouts

All scouts and adult participants attending any overnight activity — including campouts — must have a completed Annual Health and Medical Record (Parts A & B) on file. This form covers emergency contact information, medical history, and any medications or allergies your scout may have.

Complete the form and bring a signed copy to your den leader before the event. A new form is required each program year.
