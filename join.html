Ready to Join the Adventure?

Pack 149 welcomes youth in Kindergarten through 5th grade. Joining takes just two steps — and we'll be with you every step of the way.

How to Join

Two Simple Steps

1

Do This First

Register with Pack 149

Fill out our pack registration form so our leadership knows you're joining. This lets us add you to our communications, assign your scout to the right den, and make sure you're ready for your first meeting.

Register with Pack 149 ›
2

Then Do This

Register Nationally with BSA

Complete the official BSA national registration at my.scouting.org. This enrolls your scout with the Boy Scouts of America, provides insurance coverage, and makes them an official member of the Scouting program.

Register with BSA ›

Have Questions?

Our Cubmaster Stefan Bader is happy to answer any questions about the pack, dues, uniforms, or what to expect at your first meeting.
