NASA Overnight

Bear scouts spend the night at Space Center Houston, the official NASA visitor center for Johnson Space Center. Scouts sleep among historic spacecraft and rockets, explore mission control exhibits, and take part in hands-on STEM activities inspired by real space exploration. An unforgettable experience that connects scouting values to the frontier of human discovery.

All Scouts

Lexington

Pack 149 scouts spend the night aboard the USS Lexington, a World War II aircraft carrier and museum ship permanently docked in Corpus Christi, Texas. Known as "The Blue Ghost," the ship hosted overnight programs where scouts sleep in authentic Navy bunks on the hangar deck, tour the flight deck, explore historic aircraft, and learn about the carrier's storied wartime history. A truly one-of-a-kind adventure that brings history to life.
