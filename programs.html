Pack Activities

A full year of adventures, traditions, and memories for scouts and families.

The events below reflect our typical program year and the months they generally occur. Exact dates change each scouting year — check the Pack Calendar for this year's schedule.

Back to School Event

Capture the Flag

Kick off the scouting year with a pack-wide game of Capture the Flag. Scouts from all dens come together to reconnect with friends, welcome new scouts, and fire up their competitive spirit heading into the year ahead.

Popcorn Sales

Pack 149's annual popcorn fundraiser supports the pack program throughout the year. Scouts learn salesmanship and goal-setting while raising funds for activities, equipment, and campouts. Prizes are awarded based on sales totals.

Physical Challenge

New family recruitment night combined with a physical fitness challenge for returning scouts. Prospective families learn what Pack 149 is all about while current scouts test their strength, speed, and agility in a fun competition.

Bobcat Ceremony

Every new scout's first milestone. The Bobcat ceremony celebrates scouts who have completed the Bobcat Adventure — their introduction to the Scout Oath, Scout Law, and the spirit of Scouting. A proud moment for scouts and families alike.

Adventure NOW Campout

Formerly Webelos Woods

An overnight campout exclusively for 4th and 5th grade scouts. Webelos and Arrow of Light scouts camp alongside Scouts BSA troops, building outdoor skills and getting a real preview of what's waiting for them after Cub Scouts.

All Scouts

Fall Campout

The whole pack heads outdoors for an overnight family camping experience. Scouts and families set up camp, cook outdoors, explore trails, and spend a night under the Texas stars together. One of the most anticipated events of the year.

Photo Coming Soon
All Scouts

Holiday Bazaar

One of Pack 149's most beloved traditions. Scouts and families come together before the winter break to celebrate with games, crafts, food, and holiday fun. A great way to close out the fall semester and build pack community.

January
Photo Coming Soon
Arrow of Light

AOL & Crossover Ceremony

The highlight of the scouting year for 5th grade scouts. Arrow of Light scouts earn their final Cub Scout rank and ceremonially cross a bridge into a Scouts BSA troop, beginning the next chapter of their Scouting journey. An emotional and memorable milestone for the whole family.

Photo Coming Soon
All Scouts

Pinewood Derby

The most anticipated event of the year. Scouts design, build, and race their own wooden cars down a regulation track. Competition heats, trophies, and plenty of family cheering make this a Pack 149 classic. The cars — and the creativity — never disappoint.

Webelos I Campout

A dedicated overnight campout for 4th grade Webelos scouts. With Arrow of Light on the horizon, this campout focuses on outdoor skills, teamwork, and preparing scouts for the challenges ahead in their final Cub Scout year.

Lexington

Pack 149 joins packs from across the Chisholm Trail District for a day of scout skills competitions and outdoor activities. A great opportunity for scouts to test what they've learned all year and represent Pack 149 with pride.

Blue & Gold Ceremony

The pack's annual awards banquet celebrating another year of achievements. Rank advancements, adventure badges, and special recognitions are presented in a festive setting with the whole pack community gathered together.

Cubmaster Challenge

A pack-wide skills competition to close out the program year. Scouts are tested on what they've learned: navigation, knots, outdoor skills, teamwork, and more. A fun send-off that celebrates the growth scouts have made over the year.
