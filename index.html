WELCOME TO

CUB SCOUT
PACK 149

Pack 149 has been calling Spicewood Elementary home for over 40 years. Chartered by the Northwest Austin Rotary Club, we're one of the largest and most active packs in the Chisholm Trail District. Through camping trips, hands-on challenges, and beloved annual traditions, we help youth in Kindergarten through 5th grade develop character, confidence, and a love of the outdoors.

  • 📅
    Pack Meetings
    One Tuesday per month in the Spicewood Elementary cafeteria
  • 👥
    Den Meetings
    1–2 times per month during the school year
  • 📍
    Chartered By
    Northwest Austin Rotary Club · Chisholm Trail District · Capitol Area Council
